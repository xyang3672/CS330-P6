Wireframe Prototype

A wireframe prototype that redesigns a fictional online book club app, which allows users to maintain "bookshelves" of books that they want to read, are reading now, and that they've finished reading. They should be able to add and remove books from the shelves, or move them from one shelf to another.

iPod Music Player

A stimulation of ipod music player that has implementatatio of a two-screen music player interface showing an album and song select screen. This first screen contains the album covers of four different albums. When first album is selected, a page of list of songs will display, which can jump to a selected song.
